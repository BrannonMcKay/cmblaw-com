About Patent Portfolios

A patent portfolio is the collection of patents and patent applications owned by a single entity — whether a corporation, university, or individual inventor. Analyzing a portfolio reveals an organization's innovation strategy, technical focus areas, and intellectual property strength.

This tool queries the USPTO's Open Data Portal to provide a visual overview of any entity's patent activity. Patent data shown includes utility patents, design patents, and plant patents filed with the United States Patent and Trademark Office.
